Some of My Favorite Pilates Spots in NYC

Natural Pilates

Natural Pilates is a wonderful studio - but I would only recommend this studio for those who have Reformer Pilates experience and are comfortable on the machine. It's a fast-paced class and you should definitely be comfortable with cueing and Pilates terminology. I really enjoy all the different classes they have and I definitely feel the burn in every class.

Natural Pilates Website

Fuze House

Fuze House is specifically a mat hot Pilates studio- the heat definitely adds an ectra spicy element that I love. I love the incorporation of props (like weights, and bands) to give an extra burn and I always leave sweaty and glowing.

Fuze House Website

NY Pilates

NY Pilates is definitely the most aesthetically pleasing studio out of all these options, and is pretty beginner friendly. While I love the studio (and it always smells good) because I have been doing Pilates for so long I don't feel the classes are as challenging as other studios but it is always great for a quick little workout to move the body.

NY Pilates Website

Club Pilates

I am currently an instructor for Club Pilates specifically and I absolutely love this studio (maybe I'm a little biased). Club Pilates is extremely injury friendly and pregnancy friendly, and truly for everybody and person. Depending on which class you take you can get a really good difficult workout- but there also slower paced classes if that's not your jam. Highly recommend you come and try us out!

Club Pilates Website
